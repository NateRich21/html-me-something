Tame Impala

Tame Impala in a Psychedelic Rock band led by frontman, Kevin Parker. This band has created some of the the most original and inovative music of the last decade. Tame Impala has three studio, LP releases through their current record label:

  1. Innerspeaker (2010)
  2. Lonerism (2012)
  3. Currents
  4. Other work

Innerspeaker

2010

Tame Impala's studio debut exploded onto the rock scene in 2010. Drawing inspiration from traditional psych-rock prisms, the band managed to not only recreate the genre's signature, vintage sound, but also reinvent it. Using current tech to add a modern twist on a vintage sound, Tame Impala created an original, brilliant piece of work.

Lonerism

2012

Lonerism is Tame Impala's exciting progression from Innerspeaker. By bringing more synths into the mix for the band's sophmore album, Kevin Parker yet again gives an exciting update to an already exciting sound. While not being anything close to an edm album, Lonerism's songs are structured like electronic music; eacha track is treated as a blank canvas to be filled with fluidity, contant motion and textural evolution. Parker also gives a taste of affinity for writing brilliant, yet catch melodies throughout the body of work.

Currents

2015

Currents is radically different from the band's other LPs. Kevin Parker personally wrote, played, recorded, produced and mixed every single note on the album. Rather than suppressing his instincts for pop melodies, Parker fully embraces them and creates a body of work that is equally accesible and brilliant. Mo-town, R&B, soul, funk, and new-wave all find themselves masterfully and delicately intertwined with psych-rock's vintage kaleidoscope of sound creating one of the most sonically thrilling and unique albums of the last ten years.

Other work

2007 - Present

Prior to their studio debut, Tame Impala had independtly released a slew of EPs and singles. They also have covered musicians such as Fleetwood Mac and Michael Jackson. Following the release of Currents, Kevin Parker became a hot commodity among the music community and has collaborated with many artist in the last few years. He was featured as a producer and vocalist on three tracks from Mark Ronson's Uptown Funk and has remixed songs for Miguel. Rihanna even covered The Currents finale, New Person, Same Old Mistakes as a main track on her most recent album. Parker produced her version as well.
